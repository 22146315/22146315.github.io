Apple phones in 2026 have reached a new peak of versatility, led by the iPhone 17 series and the revolutionary iPhone 18 Fold. The lineup ranges from the sleek, ultra-thin iPhone 17 Air to the powerhouse Pro models, which now feature under-display Face ID and advanced A19/A20 Pro chips that power sophisticated on-device AI.
